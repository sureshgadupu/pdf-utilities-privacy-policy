# Privacy Policy for PDF Utilities

## Information Collection and Use

PDF Utilities does **not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information** directly.

The app uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information (such as device identifiers, IP address, or ad interaction data) to provide and improve advertising services, in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

## Data Security

PDF Utilities does not store any user data on its own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is handled according to their respective privacy policies.
